SiteSnap
Made this for you —
for snag lists, jobs, whatever you like.
Keep all your site photos and notes in one place. Make a PDF to share with a client. All from your phone.
1
Main screen
Your jobs live here. Tap + New Site to add one.
2
Add your job details
Give the job a name, client and address. Add any notes you like. Tap Save.
3
Take photos on site
Tap Camera at the bottom — photos go straight into whichever job you're on.
4
Inside a job
Add more photos anytime, or tap Add Voice Note to record something you can play back later.
5
Generate a PDF
Tap Generate PDF — makes a document with all your photos and notes. Share or email it straight from here.
